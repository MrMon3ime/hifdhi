1 Introduction & Roles

Hifdhi helps teachers and administrators run Qur'an memorization circles: enrolling students, tracking attendance, logging memorization and revision sessions, and producing reports.

Admin: sees all students, circles, and teachers; can manage accounts and reset data.
Sheikh (Teacher): sees only their own students and circles; records their attendance, sessions, and reports.

2 Signing In

  1. Open the app to reach the login screen.
  2. Enter the email and password provided by your admin.
  3. Tap Sign In.
  4. To switch language, tap the globe icon 🌐 at the top of the card (English / عربي).
Note: If you see "Invalid email or password", double-check your credentials or contact your admin.

3 Dashboard

Shows today's summary in quick cards:

  • Total Students and active count.
  • Present Today with attendance rate.
  • Absent Today.
  • Verses Memorized Today and number of sessions.

Circle stats: use the dropdown above the cards to pick a specific circle (e.g. the Fajr circle) or "All circles". Because a student may attend at more than one time, the present/absent figures update based on the chosen circle.

The dashboard also shows weekly progress and attendance-rate charts, a Top Students list, and quick-action buttons.

4 Students

  1. Tap + Add Student and fill in: name (Arabic), name (English), gender, phone, circle, status.
  2. Use the search bar and filters to narrow the list.
  3. Each student has actions: View (full profile), Edit, Archive, Delete.
  4. Tap CSV or XLS to export the student list.

The student profile shows completion %, total verses, attendance %, current position, and a Progress Map.

Archive vs Delete: Archiving hides a student but keeps their data; deleting permanently removes them along with their sessions and attendance.

5 Attendance

  1. Choose the circle and date at the top.
  2. For each student pick a status: Present / Late / Excused / Absent.
  3. Use Mark all to set one status for everyone.
  4. Tap Save Attendance at the bottom.
Important: Record attendance per circle so the dashboard stats stay accurate (e.g. Fajr attendance separate from another circle).

6 Qur'an Progress & Sessions

  1. Select a student from the side list.
  2. Tap Record New Session.
  3. Choose the type: New Memorization or Revision.
  4. Set the range From and To (surah and ayah number); you can clear a number and type a new one freely.
  5. Add a star rating and notes, then tap Save.

The app automatically counts the ayahs and updates the student's current position and total memorized. Sessions appear in the timeline and can be edited or deleted.

Admin: "Reset All" zeroes every student's progress and deletes all sessions — use with care.

7 Progress Map (Juz / Hizb / Thumn)

In the Juz Map tab (and the student profile) you can switch the view:

UnitCountDescription
Juz30The Qur'an divided into thirty parts
Hizb60Two hizb per juz
Thumn480Eight thumn per hizb

Completed cells turn green based on the student's current memorization position.

8 Muraja'ah (Revision)

Lists each student's revision sessions with status (completed / pending / missed / partial) and rating. Use the filters to pick a student or status.

9 Matn (Texts)

  1. Tap Add Matn and choose the student and text type (Bayquniyyah, Jazariyyah, Ajurrumiyyah…).
  2. Set the chapter, progress %, and notes.
  3. Matn cards show a progress bar and can be deleted.
Note: Matn data is stored locally on the device.

10 Analytics

Comprehensive stats: total students, total verses memorized, average attendance, graduates, plus charts for weekly verses, monthly attendance, student status distribution, and student ranking.

11 Reports & Export

  1. Pick the date range (from / to).
  2. Choose a report: Attendance, Progress, Revision, or Comprehensive.
  3. Tap PDF, Excel, or CSV to export.

Reports export with proper Arabic headers and direction. On desktop a print dialog opens to save as PDF; on mobile the file is shared.

12 Halaqat (Circles)

  1. Tap Add Circle and enter the name (AR/EN), teacher, location, and status (active/inactive).
  2. Circles can be edited or deleted. Deleting detaches students without deleting them.

13 Teachers & Accounts (Admin)

  1. In Teachers, tap Add Account.
  2. Enter name, email, password, and role (Sheikh / Admin).
  3. Each account card shows its circles and students count and can be edited or deleted.

14 Settings

  • Account: view name, email, and role.
  • Language: switch between Arabic and English.
  • Theme: light / dark / system.
  • Security: change password.
